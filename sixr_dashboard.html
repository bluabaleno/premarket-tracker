Resolution Rules

Market resolves YES if the specified team is declared the official winner. This includes wins via:
• Standard result  •  Super Over  •  Multiple Super Overs  •  DLS method

Market resolves NO in all other cases:
• Tie (group stage)  •  No Result / Abandoned  •  Match not completed by deadline

Resolution source: Official ICC match results via ESPN Cricinfo
